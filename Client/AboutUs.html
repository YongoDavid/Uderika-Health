Our story

Introduction:

At uderika, our journey began with a vision and a passion for providing health education to millions of Nigerians and Africans going through chronic health challenges. We saw an opportunity to impact peopleâ€™s lives by sharing medical knowledge and were determined to make a difference.

Inception:

Founded in 2023, uderika was born out frustration of the lack of patient education and counselling we witness firsthand in the Nigerian health sector. We envisioned a world where people with chronic health challenges can have a good understanding of their medical diagnosis, treatment options and medications.

Our Mission:

Our mission at uderika, is to empower people with the right medical knowledge, through easy and fun videos breaking down complex health concepts into simple analogies, so that everyone can understand. We are committed to putting clients first, fun and innovation.

Our social impact

Through our "Buy a Course, Gift a Future" program, we pledge to educate a Nigerian child for every course purchased. We believe in the transformative power of education to break the cycle of poverty and illness, promoting individual prosperity and national development.
